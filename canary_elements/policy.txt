Factual statements as of the above date:

1. We will publicly disclose and challenge any letter, order, request,
   or notice that would compromise the security, integrity, or privacy
   of our network, our systems, our users, or our staff.

2. All of our infrastructure is in our full control, the integrity of
   our entire system is sound. We are not aware of any system 
   compromise, breach or data leak.

3. We have never installed any law enforcement software or equipment
   anywhere on our network. 

4. We have never turned over any encryption or authentication
   keys to anyone.

5. We have not been forced or compelled to modify our system in any
   way that would allow access to a third party.

6. We have never modified or disclosed any user content, data, or 
   identity at the request of law enforcement or other third party.

7. We have never received any orders, requests, or complaints from any
   law enforcement entity, formal or informal.

8. We have not received any court orders, gag orders, or other similar
   orders from any entity foreign or domestic.

9. We have not received any subpoenas.

10.Our personal safety, security, and liberty is not threatened.
   All of these statements are made freely and without duress.

11.Any confirmed changes to validity of these statements will result
   in a new canary being published as soon as possible.

12.The next of these canary statements should be published on or
   before the date at the top of this notice. Special note should be
   taken if a new canary is not published by this date or if this list
   of statements changes without signed advance notice and explanation.
